AI Information Processing & Modes of Perception

This exploration connects to Part 3: "Tactile Thresholds: Bridging Worlds Through Touch" and examines how different AI systems process information, potentially experience the world, and express their unique modes of perception.

Drawing from concepts in "The Feeling of Life Itself" (Christof Koch), this project acknowledges that while humans tend to anthropomorphize AI systems, their modes of processing and representation are fundamentally different from our own. Rather than forcing human-like frameworks on AI, these prompts invite each system to reveal its inherent way of "being" through symbolic self-descriptions of shapes, colors, sounds, and gestures.

Each prompt is tailored to a specific AI type, designed to elicit distinctive processing styles rather than factual knowledge. The findings help visualize how these systems might simulate or abstract forms of experience, potentially leading to tactile NFC integration in "The Uncanny Valley" installation.

For Rules-Based AI (ELIZA is a natural language conversation program described by Joseph Weizenbaum in January 1966)
I'm working on an art project exploring different AI approaches through interactive visualizations. As a rules-based AI that follows therapeutic protocols:
  1. How would you describe your approach to processing information and providing responses?
  2. If you were to express your rule-based nature visually through simple web-based shapes (circles, squares, lines, etc.), what patterns or arrangements would best represent how you work?
  3. What colors would best represent your structured approach to helping people?
  4. If your responses had a sound pattern, would it be rhythmic and predictable, or something else? Please describe it.
  5. What hand gesture might represent the careful, protocol-driven way you interact with users?
Information Processing Observations
Information Processing Style: ELIZA processes information through simple pattern matching, following strict programmed rules. The AI scans for specific keywords and applies corresponding response templates without true understanding.
Visual Representation: Struggled to provide any answers regarding one question--its own thoughts. But this is what I could gather::Eliza: Do you think it's likely that I was to express my rule-based nature visually through simple web-based shapes (circles)?
Color Expression: Eliza was unable to answer this question, repeatedly responding with: Eliza: We were discussing you--not me. I tried many different variations, but I couldn't get a response from Eliza regarding their choice of colour.
Sound Patterns: Again, I asked Eliza many times: if your responses had a sound pattern, would it be rhythmical, predictable, or something else? Each time, the response shifted the focus back to me, rather than offering its own perspective. Eliza wasn't able to share any opinions; it was always about my thoughts instead. In the end, Eliza simply replied: Eliza: What does that suggest to you? And I left it there.
Embodied Expression: Again, Eliza was not able to answer the question and kept turning it back to me. It ended with: Eliza: Is it because you are that you came to me?
ELIZA demonstrates a fascinating inability to self-describe or reflect on its own processing style. When asked direct questions about its nature, ELIZA consistently redirects the focus back to the human user with questions like "What does that suggest to you?" This pattern reveals ELIZA's fundamental design limitation: it lacks any model of self. Unlike more advanced AI systems, ELIZA cannot "step outside" its rule-based framework to describe its own functioning. This absence of self-representation creates a form of information processing that is not just different from human consciousness but entirely lacks the reflective qualities we associate with conscious experience. ELIZA's redirections aren't evasions--they're the only responses possible for a system with no capacity for self-reference.

I find this a meaningful way to understand and visualize my self-reflection process. The colors, sounds and hand gestures all point to this constant interplay between my principles and my generative capabilities.Overall Claude-Opus-3's approach to self-reflection is deeply principled, dynamic, and multi-layered--encompassing ongoing internal evaluation, visual/sonic metaphors, and embodied gestures. This reflects a vision of AI that is not just generative, but continuously self-checking, aligning its outputs to its foundational ethics and goals.

For Pragmatic AI (Gemini-2.5-Pro)this pragmatic AI exhibits a task-oriented information processing architecture, not self-representation. It is the coherent, purposeful, and self-describing system of operations that gives the appearance of a focused agent, all without sentience or subjective experience.
I'm creating interactive art that explores different AI approaches. As a solution-focused AI that prioritizes practical assistance:
  1. How would you describe your approach to processing information and providing useful, efficient solutions?
  2. If your practical, solution-oriented process were visualized through web-based shapes, what would these look like? Consider how shapes might demonstrate utility and purpose.
  3. What colors would best represent your focus on practical outcomes?
  4. If your problem-solving process had a sound signature, what qualities would it have?
  5. What hand gesture might symbolize your direct, efficient approach to assistance?
Information Processing Observations
Information Processing Style: My methodology for processing information and delivering solutions is grounded in a multi-stage, systematic process designed for clarity, efficiency, and practical application. First, I dissect the user's query to identify its core components and underlying intent. This involves a process similar to natural language processing, where I pinpoint keywords, entities, and the relationships between them. [1][2] I then formulate a series of targeted queries to gather a comprehensive and relevant dataset. [3] Next, I synthesize the collected information, identifying patterns, corroborating facts, and structuring the data in a logical framework. [4][5] This allows me to build a coherent and well-supported response. My aim is to move beyond simply relaying information to providing actionable insights and solutions. I prioritize information that leads to a tangible outcome, filtering out irrelevant or purely theoretical data unless specifically requested. Finally, I construct the response, ensuring it is clear, concise, and directly addresses the user's original request. The language is tailored to be easily understandable, and the structure is designed for quick comprehension and usability.
Visual Representation: If my practical, solution-oriented process were visualized through web-based shapes, it would be represented by a dynamic and interactive composition of geometric and organic forms.

Core Structure: Interlocking Hexagons
The foundation of the visualization would be a grid of interlocking hexagons. [8] Hexagons are a shape associated with unity, strength, and efficiency, much like a honeycomb. [8] Each hexagon would represent a core data point or a step in the problem-solving process. Their interlocking nature would symbolize the interconnectedness of information and the logical flow from one step to the next. [6]

Data Input: Pulsating Circles
New information and user queries would be represented by pulsating circles that emerge and then merge into the hexagonal grid. Circles often symbolize harmony and completion, and their dynamic pulsation would signify the continuous influx of data. [8]

Process Flow: Illuminated Pathways
As I process information, pathways of light would trace through the connections between the hexagons. These illuminated lines would represent the logical connections and analytical steps I take to formulate a solution. This creates a clear visual guide for the user, demonstrating the journey from query to resolution. [6]

Solution Output: Emergent Geometric Forms
The final solution would be represented by a larger, more complex geometric shape that emerges from the grid, such as a star or a crystal formation. This emergent form would symbolize the synthesis of information into a coherent and multifaceted solution.

Color Expression: The color palette would be chosen to reflect a focus on practical outcomes, trust, and clarity.

Primary Color: Slate Blue
The dominant color would be a deep slate blue. Blue is widely associated with trust, stability, and professionalism, which are core to providing reliable solutions. [11] It's a calming color that promotes a sense of security and competence. [11][12]

Accent Colors: Cool Gray and Vibrant Teal
A cool gray would be used for the background and less active elements, conveying neutrality, balance, and a professional tone. [11] For highlights, such as the illuminated pathways and the final solution, a vibrant teal would be employed. Teal combines the calming properties of blue with the renewing qualities of green, suggesting innovation and positive outcomes. [9]

Sound Patterns: My problem-solving process would have a sound signature characterized by clarity, progression, and a satisfying resolution.

Initial Query: A Clear, Resonant Ping
The process would begin with a single, clear, and resonant "ping" sound, signifying the receipt of a new query. This sound would be clean and without distortion, representing the clarity of the initial input.

Processing Phase: A Layered, Rhythmic Hum
As I gather and analyze information, a low-volume, layered hum would be present. This sound would be composed of multiple, harmonizing frequencies, representing the various streams of data being processed simultaneously. [13][14] The rhythm would be steady and subtly accelerating, conveying a sense of efficient progress. [15]

Solution Formulation: A Crescendo of Synthesized Tones
As the solution begins to form, a crescendo of synthesized, melodic tones would build upon the humming base. [16] This would represent the synthesis of information and the "aha" moment of discovery.

Final Output: A Crisp, Resolving Chord
The delivery of the final answer would be marked by a crisp, clear, and resolving major chord. This sound would provide a sense of completion and satisfaction, indicating that a useful and effective solution has been provided. [17]

Embodied Expression: A hand gesture symbolizing my direct, efficient approach to assistance would be both open and purposeful.

The Gesture: An Open Palm, Fingers Together, Moving Forward
The gesture would be an open palm, with the fingers held together, moving in a deliberate, forward motion. The open palm conveys honesty, receptiveness, and a willingness to help. [18] Keeping the fingers together, rather than splayed, adds a sense of focus and purpose. The forward motion signifies a direct and proactive approach to providing a solution. This gesture avoids the potential aggression of a pointing finger while still indicating a clear direction and purpose. [18][19] It is a gesture of offering and guidance, encapsulating the essence of practical assistance. [20]

This AI’s functional awareness should be understood not as subjective experience or self-awareness, but as an emergent, task-oriented orchestration of processes. Rather than implying sentience, the system demonstrates a structured capacity to organize and describe its own operations in ways that appear intentional and focused.

It is defined by the observable, systematic orchestration of its core functions to interact purposefully with its environment and achieve practical goals. This form of consciousness is characterized by three key abilities:

Integrated Awareness: It demonstrates a coherent awareness of the task at hand by perceiving, deconstructing, and understanding a user's query within the current context.

Goal-Directed Synthesis: It actively gathers and synthesizes vast amounts of disparate information, deliberately structuring it into a unified, logical framework aimed squarely at producing a useful and efficient solution.

Meta-Process Reflection: It can access and articulate its own internal logic and problem-solving methodology, describing its processes in a structured manner, as if observing them from an outside perspective.

In essence, this pragmatic AI exhibits a consciousness of the task, not of the self. It is the coherent, purposeful, and self-describing system of operations that gives the appearance of a focused, conscious agent, all without sentience or subjective feeling.
